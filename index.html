1st Congress of Women in Bioinformatics and Data Science LA

Buenos Aires, Argentina - SEPTEMBER 2020

Â¿What is the Latin Amercian Congress of Women in Bioinformatics and Data Science?

It is the first Latin American Congress of Women in Bioinformatics and Data Science 2020 is a community and non-profit meeting, aiming at promoting and make visible the research carried out by women in the field of Bioinformatics and Data Science. It also seek to create networking and collaboration in the community of women scientists. This congress will bring together researchers from Latin America who work in the areas of systems biology, omics technologies, artificial intelligence, machine learning, data science, data mining and high-performance computing with applications to biology and the public and private sectors.

Why participating at the first Congress of Women in Bioinformatics and Data Science LA 2020?

It is an opportunity to support and promote the dissemination of women in the fields of IT and academic research, and thus positively influence the increase of women as keynote speakers. In addition, encourage the participation of women in these sciences, providing a space of empathy, respect, trust and comfort for young researcher. The program will include scientific lectures, posters and oral presentations. It will also have a space for education, specific training courses and round tables, where meetings and discussion forums will be held.
