Registration

The 3rd Women in Bioinformatics & Data Science LA Conference is a free and virtual event open to the entire community, which brings together professionals, students, academics, businesswomen, public officials and hobbyists.

The main purpose of the meeting is to promote and make visible the research carried out by women and dissidents in the field of Bioinformatics and Data Science. The whole conference will be held in English.

All the attendees will recieve Virtual Gifts. Don't forget to register to the conference!
