The 1st WBDS LA Camp The WBDS Camp is free intensive training for early-career researchers, undergraduate, and graduate students interested in learning open Bioinformatics and Data Science from scratch.

Register

Terms & Conditions

Aiming to reduce the gender gap in the areas of Bioinformatics and Data Science, the 1st WBDS Camp "Introduction to Bioinformatics and Data Science" was born from the hand of WBDS LA, which consists of develop a training that allows acquiring the necessary tools to take the first steps in the aforementioned areas. To meet this objective, the WBDS LA Camp will have no cost for those who participate (scholarships of 100% of the cost of the course will be awarded) and the dictation of the same will adopt the virtual and asynchronous modality with pre-scheduled tutoring meetings in all its units.
