Upcoming Cohort

The WBDS LA Camp is a free intensive training for early-career researchers, undergraduate, and graduate students interested in learning Bioinformatics and Data Science from scratch.

Register

Terms & Conditions

Aiming to reduce the gender gap in the areas of Bioinformatics and Data Science, the 1st WBDS LA Camp "Introduction to Bioinformatics and Data Science" is being organized by the WBDS LA. This event aims to provide training for students to acquire the necessary tools to take their first steps in the aforementioned areas. To fulfill this objective, the WBDS LA Camp will have no cost for those who participate (scholarships of 100% will be awarded) and it will be virtual. All lessons will be asynchronous, but pre-scheduled tutoring meetings will be offered in all units.
