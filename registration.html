The COVID-19 pandemic has made our face-to-face meeting model unfiseable, as the authorities have imposed travel restrictions and physical estrangement that are increasingly strict and for an indeterminate time.

In this time of uncertainty, we seek to turn the crisis into an opportunity. This is why the organizing committee of the 1st Latin American Congress of Women in Bioinformatics and Data Sciences has decided that the best option is to move to a virtual version of the congress. We prioritize participants' health without losing the possibility of making visible the work that we all carry out in the different areas of knowledge that concern us.

FAQs Virtual Poster Session 2020

Who can send an abstract?

This event is open to the entire community, anyone can send an abstract for the poster session and be selected for oral presentation.

How will be the selection for oral presentation?

All abstracts will be reviewed by 2 specialists in the field. According to their recommendations, the Scientific Committee will determine if the abstract is presented as oral or poster.

Which will be the virtual poster session format?

Virtual poster sessions will be done through a 10-minute live presentation in a virtual room. Every room will count with a host and a moderator. At the end of the presentations, 5 minutes discussions will be allowed in small groups.

How is the format poster?

Participants will receive from the official email account a template PowerPoint slide.
